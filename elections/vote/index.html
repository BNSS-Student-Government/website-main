How to Vote
Voting will take place on Friday, April 8th, 2022.

A reminder that all voting for the student government election this year will be held in person. This page is for informational purposes only.

Learn about the Candidates

  Students will have complete access to all the candidate profiles on the website, and should familiarize themselves with the candidates before election week. Candidates will also be campaigning throughout the school and engaging with the student body.

Our Voting System

  We will be using the ranked-choice voting system for the 2022 election. Students will be asked to rank the top 5 candidates for each candidate. Please watch the video for more information about ranked-choice voting. See a sample ballot here.


Important! If you are unable to attend class on Friday morning, an early-voting session will be available the day before (Thursday, April 7th) after school in Room 211. Please bring a copy of your schedule and student ID.
